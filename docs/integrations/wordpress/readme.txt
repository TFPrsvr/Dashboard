== Installation ==

= Automatic Installation =

1. Log in to your WordPress admin dashboard
2. Navigate to Plugins → Add New
3. Search for "PassItOn Donation Widget"
4. Click "Install Now" and then "Activate"
5. Go to PassItOn → Settings to configure your first widget

= Manual Installation =

1. Download the plugin zip file
2. Upload the zip file through Plugins → Add New → Upload Plugin
3. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
4. Go to PassItOn → Settings to configure your first widget

= After Installation =

1. **Create Account**: Click "Connect to PassItOn" and create your free account
2. **Verify Email**: Check your email and verify your account
3. **Configure Widget**: Set up your first donation widget with our wizard
4. **Add to Site**: Use the Gutenberg block, shortcode, or widget area
5. **Test Donation**: Make a test donation to ensure everything works
6. **Go Live**: Start accepting real donations immediately

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Is PassItOn free to use? =

Yes! PassItOn offers a free plan that includes 1 donation widget and up to 50 donations per month. We only charge standard payment processing fees (2.9% + $0.30 per transaction). Paid plans offer additional features and higher limits.

= What payment methods do you support? =

We support all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover) through our secure Stripe integration. ACH bank transfers and digital wallets like Apple Pay and Google Pay are also supported.

= Do I need to handle tax receipts? =

No! PassItOn automatically generates and emails tax-deductible receipts to all donors. All receipts include the necessary information for tax purposes and comply with IRS requirements.

= Can I customize the appearance of donation widgets? =

Absolutely! Our widget customization system allows you to match your site's branding perfectly. Change colors, fonts, button text, layout, and more. Advanced users can add custom CSS for unlimited flexibility.

= Is my data secure? =

Yes. PassItOn is PCI DSS compliant and uses bank-level encryption. We never store credit card information on our servers, and all data is transmitted securely using SSL encryption.

= Can I use PassItOn with my existing theme? =

Yes! PassItOn is designed to work seamlessly with any WordPress theme. Our widgets are responsive and will adapt to your theme's styling automatically.

= Do you support recurring donations? =

Yes! Donors can choose to make one-time or recurring monthly donations. Recurring donors receive automatic receipts and can manage their subscriptions through a donor portal.

= Can I track donation analytics? =

Yes! Every PassItOn account includes detailed analytics showing donation trends, donor demographics, conversion rates, and more. Export data for tax reporting or further analysis.

= Is there a limit on donation amounts? =

No, there are no limits on individual donation amounts. However, very large transactions may require additional verification for security purposes.

= Can I use multiple widgets on one site? =

Yes! The number of widgets depends on your plan:
- Free: 1 widget
- Professional: 5 widgets  
- Enterprise: Unlimited widgets

= Do you provide customer support? =

Yes! All users receive email support. Professional and Enterprise customers get priority support with faster response times. We also provide extensive documentation and video tutorials.

= Can I use PassItOn for political campaigns? =

PassItOn is designed for charitable giving and nonprofit fundraising. For political campaign donations, please consult with legal counsel to ensure compliance with campaign finance laws.
